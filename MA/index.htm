Welcome to Math Analysis

This 12-week course will cover a range of topics including Algebra 2, Precalculus, and Differential Calculus. Here are some guidelines for using your calculator throughout the course:

  • Ensure your calculator is in the correct mode (degree/radian) as required.
  • Familiarize yourself with functions such as graphing, calculating derivatives, and solving equations.
  • Make use of memory functions to store important values during complex calculations.
  • Practice using your calculator efficiently to save time during exams and assignments.
