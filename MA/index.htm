Welcome to Math Analysis

Welcome to Math Analysis! This 12-week course will gradually cover topics commonly found in Algebra 2, Precalculus, and Differential Calculus. Be sure to watch the videos, read the materials, and complete all the assignments posted each week. Recommended calculators for this course include the:

  • TI-83
  • TI-84
  • TI-36X Pro
Please only use graphing calculators in portions of the course where it is specified. The TI-36X is permitted for use throughout the entire course.
If you have any questions, feel free to email me at support@pyaresquare.com.
